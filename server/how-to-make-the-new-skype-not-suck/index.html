Yeah, so no-one likes the new Skype, that’s ok. I don’t either, at all.

However at the minute I have to use it to record podcasts, so how can I remain sane in everyday life when I use it to talk to people at work? Easy, someone wrote an Adium plugin that powers Skype behind the scenes for you.

insta-install-a-click

With it installed just restart Adium, and add Skype to your current accounts. Skype has to be running, but you don’t have to see it unless you’re gonna do video chat.

rm -R ~/Library/Application\ Support/Adium\ 2.0/PlugIns/SkypePlugin.AdiumPlugin
